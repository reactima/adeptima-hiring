## Resume NER
* NER(Named-entity recognition) known as entity identification, 
entity chunking and entity extraction that 
seeks to locate and classify named entities in text 
into pre-defined categories: 
 - names of persons
 - organizations
 - locations
 - expressions of times
 - quantities
 - monetary values
 - percentages
